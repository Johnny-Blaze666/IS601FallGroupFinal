# Target Customer Persona
One Persona that represents one target customer that you are targeting with your archetype and methods of persuasion.
## Core Needs
    * To identify and mitigate risks across their organization. 
    * To supplement staff and enhance the internal teams effectiveness. 
## Behaviors
    * Internal Staff needs training or lacking knowledge in Penetration test, SIEM implemenation, Vulnerability Management, Incident Response, etc.
    * Organization is in need of temporary Cyber Security staff for project implementation. 
    * Employees lack awareness of Cyber Risks and unwittingly engage in behavior that poses a threat to the organization.
